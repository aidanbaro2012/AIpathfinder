How Neural Networks Learn

AI models like ChatGPT use neural networks that work similarly to our brains. They have layers of "neurons" that pass information to each other.

When you ask a question, the AI processes it through these layers, with each layer understanding different aspects of your request.

Lesson: How to Talk to AI Effectively
Progress: 1/5

The key to getting good results from AI is learning how to ask the right questions. This is called "prompt engineering."

Good prompts are specific, clear, and provide enough context for the AI to understand what you want.

Try It Yourself: Improve This Prompt
"Write about AI"

This prompt is too vague. The AI doesn't know what kind of writing you want, who it's for, or how long it should be.

"Write a 300-word blog post introduction about AI for small business owners. Explain how AI can save them time on administrative tasks. Use a friendly, encouraging tone."

This prompt gives clear instructions about length, audience, content, and tone.
